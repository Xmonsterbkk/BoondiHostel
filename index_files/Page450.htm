Laundry Services Nearby:
There are washing and drying machines located about 20 meters from our hostel. Washing costs 20 baht for loads under 10 kg and 30 baht for loads over 10 kg, with a wash cycle taking around 1 hour. The dryer costs 1 baht per minute.
